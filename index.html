UK Jobs

A research tool for exploring 738 occupations from the National Careers Service, enriched with employment projections from Working Futures 2035 (DfE / Warwick IER). Each rectangle is one occupation. Area is proportional to number of people employed. Colour indicates the selected metric — toggle between AI exposure, job outlook, growth rate, and pay. Hover for details. Click to open the full profile. AI exposure scores (0–10) estimate how much AI will reshape each role: 0 = physical/manual work with minimal AI impact, 10 = fully digital work AI can largely do today.
